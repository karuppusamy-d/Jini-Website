Customizable Design

Jini Smart Switchboards are fully customizable, so your interiors can be taken into consideration when designing them. The symbols, configuration, and color of the smart switchboard can be customized as per your needs and your home's aesthetic.

  • Customizable colors and symbols
  • Touch panel interface

  • Standard 12 amps capacity
  • App and voice control
  • Interior design integration

Technical Specifications

Jini Smart Switchboards offer variation in module sizes from 1 to 24 modules. All switches are of a standard 12 amps capacity. High current switches of 40 amps can be opted for AC, Geyser and other high current devices.

Key Specifications

Module Range 1 to 24 modules
Standard Capacity 12 amps per switch
High Current Option 40 amps for AC/Geyser
Interface Touch panel with customizable symbols
Control Methods Touch, App, Voice Commands
Compatibility Alexa, Google Home, Jini Remote
Customization Colors, symbols, configuration
Installation Standard electrical box mounting
