Jini Smart Home

Home is the beginning of your journey and place of your destination. With Jini Home, we personalize technology to enhance your home comfort, design and security.

Explore

Aesthetic switches

Jini smart home gives you the flexibility to choose any color and design for your switchboards. Let the switches blend perfectly with your interior. No need to cover your switches behind curtain. Let them show off.

Timer switches

Schedule your electrical appliances by setting timers from your mobile phone.

  • Now you can turn on water heater for 15 mins in the morning.
  • Protect your electronic devices from overcharging.
  • You can switch on AC before you get home.
  • Switch on lights on sunset.

Intelligent motion and Occupancy Sensors

  • Never worry about leaving the lights and fan off. Once no presence is detected, the sensors will switch off the lights for you.
  • Use motion and occupancy sensor to monitor motion remotely, get notification in event of an intrusion.
  • Motion sensor will switch on lights for you when the motion is detected.

Automatic temperature control

Jini smart temperature sensor monitors and controls the air conditioner and fan temperature and speed respectively. Pre-program your home to change with the seasons or simply the time of day. And Jini will do it for you.

Energy saving

With Jini Smart home, you can get significant energy savings, and hence cost savings. Your home devices can be automatically powered down when they are not needed using mobile app, timer switches or smart sensors.

Hasstle free installation
