Sensors

Experience ultimate comfort by Jini Smart Sensors module. Monitor your home in real-time and take control of your home from anywhere.

Sit back and relax, let the smart sensors do the work for you.

IR Blaster

Replace all the traditional IR remote controls in your home. Integrate all infrared remotes to your smartphone, Alexa or Google Assistant voice control.

Motion Sensor

Lights turn on automatically when the motion is detected .You can turn on different lights during day and night for example normal lights at evening and foot lights at night. When you are away, it can act as security device and detect any intrusion in house and give alert on your phone.

Occupancy Sensor

Never worry about leaving the home devices on when you leave the room, let our smart sensor handle it. You will get an alert on your mobile when no presence is detected and devices are still on.

Temperature and Humidity Sensor

Keep your home comfortable at all times by controlling temperature and humidity automatically. You can set temperature and humidity in the mobile app and sensor will maintain the temperature by controlling Fan and AC.
